About Us

Our Story

NobleNest Daycare was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a place where every child feels genuinely safe, seen, and celebrated. What started as a small home-based program has grown into a thriving center serving over 500 families in Kyle, TX.

Our founder, Maria Santos, drew inspiration from her own experience as a parent seeking quality childcare. She envisioned a place where children weren't just watched, but actively engaged in meaningful play and learning experiences designed around their developmental needs.

Today, NobleNest Daycare stands as a testament to what's possible when passionate educators and supportive families come together around a shared commitment to childhood excellence.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, safe, and nurturing childcare that supports each child's unique development and prepares them for lifelong success and happiness.

15 Years of Excellence

Serving families in Kyle, TX with dedicated, compassionate care since 2010.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at NobleNest Daycare.

Safety

Every child's physical and emotional safety is our top priority. We maintain rigorous safety protocols and create trauma-informed environments.

Respect

We honor each child's individuality, cultural background, and unique learning style. Every voice matters in our community.

Learning

We foster curiosity and confidence through play-based education rooted in best practices in child development.

Joy

Childhood is meant to be joyful. We create an environment where laughter, play, and genuine happiness thrive daily.

Community

We believe strong families create strong communities. We're committed to partnership with families and local organizations.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in early childhood education through continuous professional development and innovation.
